Urban Spin Laundry

Website Planning Document

Site Name

The site name is Urban Spin Laundry. The name represents a modern, city focused laundry service. The word “Spin” directly relates to laundry services, while “Urban” positions the business as convenient and practical for busy urban customers.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide customers with clear information about Urban Spin Laundry services. The site will explain available laundry services, pricing, turnaround time, business hours, and contact information. It will also describe how the laundry process works so customers know what to expect before using the service.

Scenarios

  • How long will my laundry take and what is the turnaround time?
  • What laundry services are offered and how much do they cost?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Deep Navy Blue – used for headings, navigation, and footer.

Secondary Color: Light Gray – used for page backgrounds and content sections.

Accent Color: Fresh Green – used for highlights and call to action elements.

Typography

Poppins: Used for headings.

Open Sans: Used for body text and paragraphs.

Wireframes

Mobile View

  • Header with logo and menu icon
  • Hero section with business name and tagline
  • Services overview section
  • Pricing and turnaround time section
  • Contact information
  • Footer with business hours

Desktop View

  • Header with logo on the left and navigation on the right
  • Hero section with call to action
  • Services displayed in columns
  • Pricing and process sections
  • Contact section
  • Footer with business details
